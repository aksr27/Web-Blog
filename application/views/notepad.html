Notepad App

We Are Future Engineers and tech lovers,We love to share our knowledge with everyone.This is our Blog and we try to solve the small problems by sharing knowledge with you all.

Hello Guys, In this tutorial ,we are going to learn how to create a Simple Notepad Application. This Tutorial will contain tha following files in our project.
1. Manifest.xml
2. MainActivity.java
3. EditActivity.java
4. NewActivity.java
5. DisplayActivity.java
6. Database.java
7. toolbar_layout.xml
8. activity_main.xml
9. activity_edit.xml
10. activity_new.xml
11. activity_display.xml

AndroidManifest.xml

< ?xml version="1.0" encoding="utf-8" ?>
<manifest xmlns:android="http://schemas.android.com/apk/res/android"
    package="com.example.anuprathore.texteditor">

    <application
        android:allowBackup="true"
        android:icon="@mipmap/ic_image"
        android:label="@string/app_name"
        android:roundIcon="@mipmap/ic_image"
        android:supportsRtl="true"
        android:theme="@style/NoActionBar">
        <activity
            android:name=".MainActivity"
            android:label="@string/app_name"
            android:theme="@style/NoActionBar">
            <intent-filter>
                <action android:name="android.intent.action.MAIN"/>

                <category android:name="android.intent.category.LAUNCHER"/>
            </intent-filter>
        </activity>
        <activity android:name=".DisplayActivity"/>
        <activity android:name=".EditActivity"/>
        <activity android:name=".NewActivity"></activity>
    </application>

</manifest>
